Welcome to The Shard!

On this page you will find out anything you want to know about this wonderful landmark!

Located next to borough market, it is in the heart of london, a few seconds away from the river Thames.
These view can be seen from the west wing on any night!

Made up of 95 floors, from the fifth floor upwards you can catch eclectic views of the city and beyond!. Currently developing the south Wing extension into Weston Street. Project completion anticipated for March 2016!.

Currently backed by capital investors, the development project is still open for further investment.

Fact Sheet

  • Floor Count: 95
  • Elevator count: 10
  • Floor Area: 110,000m2 or 1,200,000m2
  • Construction Start Date: March 2009
  • Construction completion: March 2012
